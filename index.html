About Us

At RK Bricks and Industries, we specialize in manufacturing premium red clay bricks using traditional methods like Hawa Bhatta (fan-powered firing) for extended burn time to ensure uniform sreanth and durability. We enhance our clay with Pakhmel Machine to ensure uniform quality and high strength.

Our bricks are known for their strength, durability, and wire-cut precision — ideal for all types of construction.
